The survey and meeting are open to **anyone who travels through/on 34th St** in Grays Ferry by any transportation mode \- you do not need to live within Grays Ferry, just need to travel through it\! *This is a key opportunity to demonstrate support for safety on this high-stress corridor – use the survey and meeting to ask for protected bike lanes\!*

**Public Meeting Details:**   
Monday, September 22nd | 6:00 \- 8:00 PM   
Location: [Finnegan Recreation Center](https://maps.app.goo.gl/9cfKUhvNyqkz4T8J8) (1231 S 30th St)
